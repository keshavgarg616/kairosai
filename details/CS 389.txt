CS 389: Introduction to Machine Learning<br>
Prerequisites: COMPSCI 220 (or COMPSCI 230), COMPSCI 240 (or STATISTC 315/515), and MATH 233, all with a grade of C or better.<br>
Course Details: Introduction to machine learning algorithms and applications, intended for students with no prior machine learning experience. Covers supervised learning, reinforcement learning, and real-world considerations like ethics, safety, and fairness. Topics include linear and non-linear regression, gradient descent, neural networks, classification, Markov decision processes, temporal difference learning, and ethics. Counts as an Elective for the CS and INFORM Majors. 3 credits.
