Course Details:
  Introduction to C data representation, modern architectures, assembly languages, and operating system services (I/O, process, and synchronization).<br>

Prerequisites:
  CICS 210 (or COMPSCI 187) with a grade of C or better.<br>
  COMPSCI 198C. <br>
  Equivalent experience in the C programming language is accepted.<br>

Additional Notes:
  The course focuses on the scientific principles behind the construction of high-performance, scalable systems.<br>
  Assumes students have some knowledge in the C programming language.<br>
  4 credits.<br>
  Offered in Fall and Spring. <br>
