*   Title: Reasoning Under Uncertainty<br>
*   Instructor: Mark Wilson<br>
*   Credits: 4<br>
*   Prerequisites: CICS 160 (previously INFO 190T or COMPSCI 187) or CICS 210 and MATH 132, all with a grade of C or better.<br>
*   Course Details: Development of mathematical reasoning skills for problems that involve uncertainty. Concepts are illustrated with real-world examples and exercises. Topics include counting and probability, probabilistic reasoning, and statistical topics (estimation of parameters and linear regression, as time permits).<br>
*   Frequency: Fall and Spring
